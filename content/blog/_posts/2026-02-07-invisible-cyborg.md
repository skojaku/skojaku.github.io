## Building my parts

I was daydreaming while driving to work in January 2026, thinking that things have shifted so much in the last three years. 

It brought me back to an old thought from [Phycho-Pass](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt2379308/). The show is about a society governed by ``digital secretaries''---AI systems that don't just help you, but manage the everything of your life. In this world, people don't just use technology; they are tethered to it. And this is happening in our world too.
